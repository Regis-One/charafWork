About

Nestled in the heart of Ottawa, there is a Moroccan restaurant that promises to transport you to the bustling streets of Marrakech. The restaurant is a feast for the senses, with colorful decor and the aroma of spices wafting through the air. The menu features a variety of traditional Moroccan dishes, such as tagines, couscous, and kebabs, all made with fresh ingredients and flavorful spices. The staff is warm and welcoming, happy to guide you through the menu and make recommendations. Whether you're in the mood for a leisurely lunch or a romantic dinner, this Moroccan restaurant is sure to satisfy your cravings for delicious food and exotic ambiance.

Couscous

Couscous is a traditional North African dish made from tiny grains of semolina, typically served with stews or vegetables. It is a staple food in many countries across the region.

Tajine

Tajine is a North African dish named after the earthenware pot it is cooked in. It typically consists of slow-cooked meat and vegetables, often seasoned with spices and served with couscous or bread.
